Need a Snack?

Just finished your dinner, and craving a sweet treat? Look no further than this page. Here, you can find some great dessert options.

Click on the photo to visit the restaurant's website!

Kyo Matcha

This place specializes solely in- you guessed it- matcha! My girlfriend absolutely loves matcha, so we are regulars here. The matcha is some of the best that I've ever had, and it is always consistent. If you love green tea, this place is for you!

My order:
  • Matcha soft serve
  • Iced brown sugar matcha latte

Mango Mango

This is a newer Pittsburgh spot in Squirrel Hill. They serve many mango-centered Taiwanese deserts, but my favorite has to be the mango sticky rice! This dish is warm, savory, sweet- it has it all! This dish is amazing, and it is worth stopping here just for the sticky rice!

My order:
  • Mango sticky rice

Page's Dairy Mart

Last on the list is a Pittsburgh classic. Located on the South Side, this family-owned restaurant is rich in history. If you're craving ice cream, this place is for you. Their menu is so extensive, I always have such a hard time deciding while waiting in line. Everything is amazing. However, expect to wait in-line for a while, especially on a warm summer evening!

My order:
  • Hot apple dumpling sundae
