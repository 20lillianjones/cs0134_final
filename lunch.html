Lunch Spots

Looking to grab something quick on your lunch break, or sit down with friends for your mid-day meal? Here are some of the best, most unique places to grab a bite for lunch in the city.

Click on the photo to visit the restaurant's website!

Noodlehead

The first spot on this list, and my personal favorite is Noodlehead! Located in Shadyside, this restaurant is a Thai spot specializing in- you guessed it- noodles! The service is always quick, and you can't find a better quick Thai spot in the city. Everything is always fresh, and I crave Street Noodle #1 almost daily. Give it a try!

My order:
  • Street Noodle #1
  • Pork belly steamed buns
  • Thai iced tea

Prince of India

Next up is Prince of India located in Oakland. While I have only been here for the dinner menu once, the lunch menu cannot be beat, and me and my friends regularly visit. Why you ask? Lunch is a buffet! This spot serves fresh, authentic Indian cuisine buffet-style from 11:00AM to 2:00PM each day. The food is delicious, and the price cannot be beat!

My order:
  • Chicken tikka masala
  • Garlic naan
  • Mango lassie

The Boonseek

Last up is the Boonseek! This is a food truck that can be found all over Pittsburgh, serving Korean meals. It always brightens-up my day when I am on campus and I see the Boonseek truck parked on Fifth Avenue. For a quick bite, I definitely reccommend this place, and the people are always so nice!

My order:
  • The Boon Seeker
  • Bulgogi mando
