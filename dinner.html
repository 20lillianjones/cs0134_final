Dinner Time!

Here are my favorite dinner spots in (and around) the city. These places are great for a special occasion, and each comes with their own type of cuisine. Take a look!

Click on the photo to visit the restaurant's website!

Pusadee's Garden

This has to be my favorite restaurant on this entire page. Pusadee's Garden is an upscale dining establishment serving Thai cuisine in the Lawrenceville neighborhood. This place is amazing. From the atmosphere, to the service, to the food itself- everything is a 10! The food is absolutely delicious. Be sure to make a reservation well in advance, though, as spots fill up fast!

My order:
  • Khao yum
  • Crispy shrimp
  • Thai tea sticky rice

Yoshino

This restaurant is me and Maddie's go-to for great sushi. The rolls are always so fresh and delicious. The appetizers are great as well. Not to mention, the presentation of the food is always so beautiful and interesting! I definitely reccomend giving this spot a try.

My order:
  • Crab rangoons
  • Super Mario roll

J Cork's

To be honest, I am definitely cheating a little with this one. This spot resides closer to my hometown in Greensburg, PA. However, dinner here is so amazing each time, that it was necessary to include. This upscale restaurant serves a wide range of American fair, and it is all delicious, all the time!

My order:
  • Calamari
  • Crab cakes
