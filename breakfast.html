Breakfast in the 'Burgh

Here are some of my favorite breakfast spots in Pittsburgh. While most of the photos are
of breakfast sandwiches (my favorite), each place has their own unique menu with something for everyone.

Click on the photo to visit the restaurant's website!

Pamela's Diner

First up is Pamela's Diner. This breakfast joint has been a Pittsburgh staple since 1980. Even with multiple Pittsburgh locations, expect to experience a wait if you're visiting on a Saturday or Sunday morning. However, rest assured that the wait will be worth it! This place has excellent service, as well as delivering a classic-diner vibe! This place is truly delicious.

My order:
  • Bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich
  • Lyonnaise potatoes
  • Chocolate milk

Thyme Machine

Next up is the Thyme Machine! This is a newer Pittsburgh spot, and I only tried this for the first time last summer. This spot prides themselves in only serving Pittsburgh. They operate in different areas of the city, as this is a breakfast cart, but they can mostly be found on Liberty Avenue. Since this is a breakfast cart, expect a limited menu. However, if you are a fan of a good breakfast sandwich, this is the best I've had in Pittsburgh!

My order:
  • Bacon, egg, and cheese

Pigeon Bagels

Last on this list is Pigeon Bagels. This is a Kosher bakery serving fresh bagels in Squirrel Hill. This has to be my favorite bagel in the city. They are always fresh, and the texture is perfect. Get here quick, as they often sell-out fast, especially on the weekends!

My order:
  • Lox bagel with plain cream cheese
